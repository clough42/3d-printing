Itty Bitty Double FLEX V2 Extruder
Printable Parts
Clough42, LLC

For more information:  http://clough42.com/go/itty-bitty-double-flex-v2-extruder/

Extruder Blocks:
  Double FLex Block-175E3dLiner.STL -- For 1.75mm E3D hot ends with PTFE liner
  Double Flex Block-175Universal.STL -- For all 1.75mm hot ends (no PTFE liner)
  Double Flex Block-300Universal.STL -- For all 3mm hot ends (no PTFE liner)

Common Parts (needed for all setups):
  IdlerBlock.STL -- Hinged idler bearing block (2 needed)
  IdlerPin-15mm.STL -- Bearing pin for idler block (2 needed)
  Parametric-Pulley-80-8-hex.STL -- 80-tooth GT2 hobbed bolt drive pulley (2 needed)
  ServoShelfBracket-L-HXT900.STL -- Left-side shelf bracket with hole for HXT900 servo
  ServoShelfBracker-R-NoHole.STL -- Right-side shelf bracket
  ServoSpacer.STL -- Servo mounting spacer to provide more clearance between Z probe and hot end
  Spacer 1mm.STL -- Spacer to be installed under Hobbed bolt head (2 needed)
  Spacer 2mm.STL -- Spacer to be installed behind 80-tooth pulley (2 needed)

E3dV6 Specific Parts (needed for E3D hot ends):
  DoubleFlexGrooveMount-e3dV6.STL -- Double hot end groove mount
  FanShroud-e3dv6.STL -- Fan shroud for cooling hot ends and keeping stray air off the print
  PartCoolingDoubleFanShroud-e3dV6.STL -- Fan shroud for 40mm print cooling fan
  XCarriageShelf-e3dV6.STL -- Carriage shelf with opening for e3dV6 hot end cooling
  ZProbeArm-e3dv6.STL -- Z probe arm, longer length for e3dV6 hot ends

Hexagon Specific Parts (needed for Hexagon hot ends):
  DoubleFlexGrooveMount-Hexagon.STL -- Double hot end groove mount
  PartCoolingDoubleFanShroud-Hexagon.STL -- Fan shroud for cooling hot ends and keeping stray air off the print
  TightDoubleHexagonAOShroud.STL -- Hot end cooling fan shroud for Hexagon AO hot ends
  TightDoubleHexagonShroud.STL -- Hot end cooling fan shroud for standard Hexagon hot ends
  XCarriageShelf-Hexagon.STL -- Carriage shelf with opening for Hexagon cooling fan
  ZProbeArm-Hexagon.STL -- Z probe arm, shorter length for Hexagon hot ends

Optional Parts
  AdjustableI3VXCarriagePlate.STL -- Replacement MakerFarm i3V X Carriage plate to allow leveling with an extra eccentric cam
  I3VXCarriagePlate.STL -- Replacement for the stock MakerFarm i3V X Carriage pllate (no leveling)
